Pi-hole

Feb 4, 2017
[linux, dns, ad blocker]

Pi-Hole is a nifty service to run locally on a network, as it removes most of the ads on a network level. This means that you do not need to bother with ad blockers on each and every device on your network. It is designed to run of a Raspberry Pi, but I had much better results running the Dockerized Pi-Hole on my home network.
